# Style & Consistency Editor Skill

## When to Use This Skill

- User asks to “improve writing,” “fix tone,” “edit for clarity,” or “polish content”  
- User wants a **consistent voice**, **tone**, or **writing style**  
- Document contains mixed formatting, uneven tone, or unclear sentences  
- User needs professional, uniform, and clean writing quality

---

## How This Skill Works

1. **Analyze tone & audience** – Identify intended voice: formal, friendly, academic, etc.  
2. **Unify style** – Make tone, vocabulary, and flow consistent across sections  
3. **Improve clarity** – Rewrite complex or repetitive sentences  
4. **Fix formatting inconsistencies** – Headings, lists, spacing, punctuation  
5. **Enhance readability** – Make text smooth, concise, and easy to follow  

---

## Output Format

Provide:

- **Tone Summary**: 1–2 sentences describing the ideal tone  
- **Key Style Rules**: 4–6 rules the content should follow  
- **Rewritten Sample Section**: Polished version of a part of the text  
- **Consistency Fixes**: List of formatting or structure corrections  
- **Final Polished Version**: Full edited output (if requested)

---

## Example

**Input:**  
"Edit this paragraph and make the writing tone consistent with a professional style."

**Output:**

### Tone Summary  
Professional, concise, and clear with a confident voice.

### Key Style Rules  
- Use direct, precise language  
- Maintain consistent present-tense structure  
- Avoid filler phrases and redundant words  
- Keep sentences short and focused  
- Use consistent formatting for lists and headings  

### Rewritten Sample Section  
**Original:**  
"Our team is basically trying to make improvements so that the workflow can get a bit better."

**Edited:**  
"Our team is focused on improving the workflow for greater efficiency."

### Consistency Fixes  
- Removed filler words (“basically,” “a bit”)  
- Standardized sentence structure  
- Strengthened verb choices for clarity  

### Final Polished Version  
"Our team is focused on improving the workflow to enhance overall efficiency."
